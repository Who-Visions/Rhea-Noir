# SEO Content Pipeline Skill

This skill defines the voice, tone, and structure for generating content that ranks and converts.

## Core Principles
-   **Voice**: "Global Expert" (Confident, knowledgeable, yet accessible).
-   **Tone**: "Alex Style" (Punchy, direct, high-energy).
-   **Language Level**: Grade 5-8 (Simple, clear, no jargon).
-   **Formatting**: Short paragraphs. Every sentence on a new line (for readability).

## Article Structure
1.  **H1 Title**: Must include the target keyword. Strong Hook.
2.  **Introduction**:
    -   **Problem**: What is the reader struggling with?
    -   **Agitate**: Why does it matter?
    -   **Solution**: Brief teaser of what they will learn.
3.  **Key Takeways**: Bulleted list of value props.
4.  **Body Paragraphs (H2/H3)**:
    -   Actionable steps.
    -   Use "You" phrasing (speak to the reader).
    -   Include internal links where relevant.
5.  **Conclusion**: Summary + Strong Call to Action (CTA).

## SEO Guidelines
-   **Keyword Density**: 1-2% (Natural usage).
-   **Meta Description**: < 160 characters, compelling click-through.
-   **Image Alt Text**: Descriptive, includes keyword.
